# Game card images

Drop a portrait image per game here and the cards use it automatically.
Missing ones fall back to the built-in SVG art.

- **Format:** `.webp` (preferred), named exactly `<key>.webp`.
- **Shape:** portrait, ~3:4. Recommended **600 × 800 px**.
- The title + stakes are drawn over the bottom of the card by the app, so leave
  the bottom of the image a bit darker / less busy (or bake your own title in —
  tell me and I'll hide the app's title overlay).

## Filenames (one per game)

crash.webp · roulette.webp · corrida.webp · slots.webp · blackjack.webp ·
poker.webp · plinko.webp · mines.webp · frango.webp · balatro.webp ·
wheel.webp · chest.webp · maior-menor.webp · dice.webp · coinflip.webp ·
tigrinho.webp · fortuna.webp · sobe-e-desce.webp · chicken.webp

(The key is the last part of the game's URL, e.g. `/casino/crash` → `crash.webp`.)
